About SHINDORYU

SHIN DO RYU: abbreviated SDR is a group for the study of traditional Martial Arts, founded in 1996 by Tiberiu Szollosi in order to study and research traditional systems in Martial Arts Budo.
KENJUTSU is the art of katana.

Shindo Ryu Karate is a style that studies skills and techniques, kicks and kicks, pushes, parries, dodges, harvesting, projections, wrist levers, diversions, takeovers, strangulations, stimuli of vital points, etc., in order to cope a physical confrontation with one or more opponents using body weapons. The study in this system takes place in a school with clearly established teaching and rules, led and cared for by a teacher. The essence and content of this system go far beyond the purely practical part and include many other aspects, such as discipline, teaching, philosophy, psychology, culture, religious elements, physical health, etc.
Set of elements, principles, rules, techniques in Shindo Ryu Karate -dependent between them- form an organized whole, which puts order in a field of theoretical thinking, regulates and classifies the technical material so that the practical activity works according to the purpose.
